BSSS College

Admission Open

  • How To Apply

    As per UGC (University Grants Commission) norms, an applicant must fill an Anti-Ragging Undertaking Form before filling the online admission registration form. BSSS strictly complies with the laws framed by UGC. An applicant must fill their undertaking on any of the two websites Aman Movement or UGC Anti Ragging. The Applicant must then submit a hardcopy of the Undertaking form while submitting the online admission registration form.

  • Eligibility For Admission

    All students having 10+2 at school are eligible for admission into UG courses. Admission and allocation of subjects will be made on the basis of merit, taking into consideration the marks obtained by the candidate in the last eligibility examination and a personal interview. The college would abide by the rules and norms of the Government, University and Archdiocese of Bhopal being a minority college.

  • Documents Required for Registration and Enrollment

    Photocopy of XII Class mark sheet An Eligibility Certificate from the Barkatullah University, if the applicant is from a state other than Madhya Pradesh Migration Certificate - original - for CBSE and out of state students The date will be notified by the college.
