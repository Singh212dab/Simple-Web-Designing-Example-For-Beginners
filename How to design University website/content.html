Welcome to Delhi University

University of Delhi is known for its high standard in academics and great learned faculties. It was established in 1922. From the day it has established, it has become role- model for other universities in country. When university started only 3 colleges existed: St. Stephen, Hindu and Ramjas. University is on New Delhi campus with campus spread across 69 acre of land with beautiful natural surroundings. Delhi University Campus:- 1) North Campus 2) South Campus 3) East Campus 4) West Campus
